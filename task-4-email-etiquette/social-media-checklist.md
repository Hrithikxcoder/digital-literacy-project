
# Social Media Do’s #

1.Use strong and unique passwords

2.Keep your profile professional

3.Share useful and positive content

4.Respect others’ opinions online

5.Verify information before posting

6.Use privacy settings properly

7.Think before you post anything

8.Credit original creators when sharing content

9.Report suspicious or harmful activities

10.Maintain a positive digital presence

# Social Media Don’t #

1.Don’t share personal or sensitive information

2.Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links

3.Don’t post offensive or abusive content

4.Don’t spread fake news or rumors

5.Don’t use weak or common passwords

6.Don’t engage in cyberbullying

7.Don’t overshare your location or daily routine

8.Don’t trust unknown friend requests

9.Don’t download files from untrusted sources

10.Don’t post anything that can harm your reputation
