
Prevention Checklist: Stay Safe Online:

1.Always check the sender’s email address before clicking on any links.

2.Do not share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone.

3.Avoid clicking on suspicious or shortened links.

4.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.

5.Regularly update your passwords and use strong combinations.

6.Install apps only from official app stores (Google Play Store/App Store).

7.For UPI safety: Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money.

8.For UPI safety: Never approve “collect requests” from unknown contacts.

9.Keep your device’s antivirus and software updated.

10.Log out from shared or public computers after use.
